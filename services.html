Services

Holistic, trauma-sensitive modalities to support emotional well-being, mental clarity, physical balance, and subconscious transformation.

All sessions with Marie Hoffman, CHt, are integrative and tailored to your unique needs. Whether you're working through stress, anxiety, unwanted habits, emotional patterns, or simply seeking clarity and balance, Marie blends multiple modalities to help you achieve deep, lasting change.

Remote sessions are also available and equally effective, allowing you to receive support from the comfort of your home.

Hypnotherapy (CHt)

Hypnotherapy works directly with the subconscious mind—the part of you that stores habits, beliefs, emotions, memories, and patterns. Because of this, it is often more effective and faster than methods that rely on willpower alone.

Hypnotherapy is highly supportive for:

  • Weight reduction & maintenance (including emotional eating)
  • Smoking cessation & breaking unwanted habits
  • Reducing stress, anxiety, and overwhelm
  • Improving sleep & easing insomnia
  • Overcoming fears & phobias
  • Pain management through mind–body connection

Marie specializes in gentle subconscious work that creates rapid, meaningful internal shifts—so you can experience real change in your daily life.

EFT Tapping (Emotional Freedom Technique)

EFT is a gentle technique that combines verbal acknowledgment with tapping on specific meridian points. It helps calm the nervous system, release stuck emotions, and rewire limiting beliefs.

  • Emotional regulation & grounding
  • Reducing stress and anxiety
  • Releasing stored emotional patterns
  • Supporting trauma healing gently and safely

Accelerated Resolution Therapy (ART)

ART is a highly effective, evidence-based therapy that uses guided imagery and eye movements to help the brain reprocess difficult experiences quickly and gently—often with less emotional intensity.

  • Trauma & PTSD
  • Anxiety & panic patterns
  • Phobias & intrusive memories
  • Emotional triggers and distress

Many clients experience emotional relief in fewer sessions compared to traditional therapy methods.

Life Coaching

Life Coaching provides clarity, structure, and support as you move toward personal or professional goals. Marie integrates mindset work, accountability, and subconscious alignment for long-lasting change.

  • Clarifying goals & building confidence
  • Breaking patterns that keep you stuck
  • Navigating major life transitions
  • Creating sustainable habits and boundaries

Aromatherapy

Aromatherapy uses therapeutic-grade essential oils to support emotional balance, relaxation, energetic grounding, and nervous system regulation.

  • Custom essential oil blends
  • Support for emotional balance and grounding
  • Enhances clarity and relaxation during sessions

Acupuncture & Acupressure

These modalities focus on restoring balance to the body's energy pathways, supporting both emotional and physical well-being.

  • Pain & tension relief
  • Improved circulation & energy flow
  • Nervous system balancing
  • Support for anxiety, stress, and stagnation
