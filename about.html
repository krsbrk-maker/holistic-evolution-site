Who I Am

My name is Judy Hoffman, and I am a Certified Consulting Hypnotist dedicated to helping individuals reconnect with their inner wisdom, emotional clarity, and personal identity. My work is grounded in compassion, intuition, and a deep respect for each person’s lived experience.

I specialize in supporting individuals who are beginning again after major life changes, including those navigating life after heart valve replacement and long-term anticoagulation therapy. While my services are non-therapeutic and non-medical, I understand the emotional and spiritual impact of these journeys.

My Approach

Holistic Evolution is built on the belief that transformation begins with identity. When you reconnect with who you truly are—beyond fear, beyond limitation, beyond old patterns—you open the door to clarity, empowerment, and inner peace.

My approach blends:

  • Guided hypnotic experiences
  • Intuitive coaching
  • Symbolic and nature-based imagery
  • Dreamwork and lucid dreaming education
  • Self-regulation and grounding practices

Every session is tailored to your pace, your readiness, and your personal goals.

Who This Work Supports

This space is for individuals who are:

  • Beginning again after major life changes
  • Feeling emotionally stagnant or disconnected
  • Seeking clarity, grounding, or renewed purpose
  • Wanting to reconnect with intuition and inner strength
  • Ready to explore their identity with compassion

A Personal Invitation

You are not here by accident. If you feel called to reconnect with yourself, to find clarity, or to begin again with intention, I welcome you to explore this work.
