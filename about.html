Meet Your Practicioner


- Marie Hoffman, CHt -

Marie Hoffman, CHt

* Consulting Hypnotherapist
* Integrated Healing Arts Practitioner
*Certified Graduate of the South Western Institution of Healing Arts
Supporting whole-person healing through hypnotherapy, coaching, aromatherapy, and acupuncture/acupressure.

As an Integrated Healing Arts Practitioner, I believe that real and lasting change happens when we address the whole person—not just the symptom that shows up on the surface. In our work together, we weave together consulting hypnotherapy, life coaching, aromatherapy, and acupuncture/acupressure to support emotional, mental, and physical balance.

Sessions are gentle, collaborative, and paced according to your comfort level. You are always in control, and my role is to guide, support, and offer tools that help you reconnect with your own inner wisdom. Whether you’re navigating stress or anxiety, processing past experiences, or stepping into a new chapter of life, Holistic Evolution offers a grounded, non-judgmental space for you to land.

Many clients arrive feeling overwhelmed, stuck, or unsure of their next steps. Over time, they often describe feeling more resourced, calm, and clear—both emotionally and physically. It’s an honor to walk alongside you in that process, and to witness the small, steady shifts that lead to meaningful transformation.
