Dreamwork

Dreamwork is a gentle, intuitive way to explore your subconscious through imagery, symbolism, and emotional reflection. You are never told what your dream “means.” Instead, you discover meaning through guided awareness and personal insight.

Even a single dream fragment can offer clarity, grounding, or emotional release. Dreamwork helps you reconnect with your inner world in a safe, supportive way.

Dreamwork FAQ

Are dream fragments useful? Yes. Even a single image can be meaningful.

Do dream characters represent parts of the dreamer? Yes, symbolically.

Can dreamwork reduce anxiety? Yes. It brings awareness into the present moment.

Should a practitioner interpret dreams? No. Clients discover meaning themselves.

Lucid Dreaming Techniques

1. Intention-Based Awareness

Repeat before sleep: “Tonight, I will recognize when I am dreaming.”

2. Hand-Recognition Method

  1. Look at your hands before sleep.
  2. Repeat: “When I see my hands in a dream, I will know I am dreaming.”
  3. Recall this intention if you wake during the night.

3. Memory-Reentry (MILD)

  1. Recall your last dream.
  2. Imagine becoming lucid inside it.
  3. Affirm: “Next time I’m dreaming, I will recognize it.”

4. Daily Reality Checks

Ask throughout the day: “Am I dreaming or awake?”

5. Nap-to-Llucidity Method

  1. Wake early.
  2. Stay awake briefly.
  3. Return to sleep with intention.
